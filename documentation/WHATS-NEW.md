# What's New in Omo Naija 🎉

*Omo Naija* — "child of Nigeria." How well do you really **sabi** your slang?

A loud, pass-the-phone party game for friends and family. One team describes
Nigerian slang, food, and culture while their teammates shout out guesses before
the timer runs out. Won cards stack up per team, and a winner is crowned at the
end. Works on your phone, tablet, or computer — and once installed, it works
**fully offline**.

---

## How a game goes

1. **Set up** — add your teams (2–6), pick a timer, a mode, a skip rule, a win
   condition, and a word bank.
2. **Pass the phone** to the team whose turn it is. One person is the
   clue-giver; the rest guess.
3. **Got it!** for each correct guess (tap the button or **swipe right**) — and
   **Skip** to move on (tap or **swipe left**).
4. When the timer buzzes, you'll see what that team scored, then the phone moves
   to the next team.
5. Tap **End game** any time to jump to the winner reveal — with a podium,
   counting scores, and confetti.

There's a **How to play** screen inside the app too (button on the home screen).

---

## What you can tweak

- **Teams** — 2 to 6, each with its own colour. Rename them however you like.
- **Timer** — 30, 60, or 90 seconds per turn.
- **Modes** (it's honour-system — the app shows the rule but trusts you):
  - **Green** — describe using only 3 words + gestures.
  - **Grey** — describe however you like.
  - **Mixed** — each card tells you which.
- **Skips** — *Free* (no cost), *Limited* (3 per turn), or *Penalty* (−1 point).
- **Winning** — *Open-ended* (play until you End game), *First to a score*, or a
  *Fixed number of rounds*.
- **Sound & haptics** — toggle in Settings, or mute instantly with the 🔊 button
  in the top bar.

After a turn, on the summary screen (with **Free** skips) you can tap any card to
flip it between **won** and **skipped** if someone mis-tapped — the score updates
live.

## Word banks — including your own ✍️

Omo Naija comes with two built-in banks: **Naija Classic** and **Naija Gen-Z**.
Pick one at game setup.

### Make your own bank

Go to **Settings → Word banks → Add word bank**:

1. Give it a **name** (e.g. *Office Party*).
2. Type your **words, one per line**. You can add an optional **hint** after a
   `|` (vertical bar).
3. Tap **+ Add word bank**.

**Example — what to type in the words box:**

```
Jollof | the famous rice dish
Danfo | yellow Lagos bus
Owambe | a big lavish party
Suya | grilled spicy meat
Gele
Aso ebi
```

That makes a 6-word bank. Hints are optional — `Gele` and `Aso ebi` above have
none, and that's fine. Tip: keep words plain text (no emoji) so they show up
cleanly everywhere.

Your new bank then appears in the **Word bank** picker when you start a game, and
as the **Default word bank** option in Settings.

### Delete a bank

In **Settings → Word banks**, tap the **×** next to any bank **you created** —
you'll be asked to confirm. (The two built-in banks, Naija Classic and Naija
Gen-Z, stay put.)

## Put it on your phone 📱

Open the app's web link in your phone's browser, then choose **Add to Home
Screen**. It installs like a normal app — its own icon, full screen, and it keeps
working even with **no internet**. Your settings and any game in progress are
saved on your device, so you can close it and pick up right where you left off.
